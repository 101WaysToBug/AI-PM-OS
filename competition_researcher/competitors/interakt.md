# Competitor Brief: Interakt

Last Updated: March 2025
Analyst: Wati Product Team



## Overview

Interakt is a WhatsApp-first customer engagement platform built and operated by Jio Haptik Technologies Limited, a subsidiary of Reliance Industries (India's largest conglomerate). Haptik was acquired by Reliance Jio in April 2019 in a $100 million deal (87% stake), giving Interakt deep institutional backing and access to Jio's extensive SMB distribution network across India. Interakt was launched in 2021 specifically to serve the SMB and MSME segment, positioning itself as a full-stack WhatsApp platform for personalized campaigns, sales CRM, and customer support automation.

Interakt targets primarily Indian D2C and ecommerce brands, claiming 10,000–50,000+ SMB customers on the platform (various sources cite different figures: 5,000+ D2C brands, and Haptik's 2025 "AI for All" initiative announcement referenced 50,000 existing Interakt SMB users). The platform is the #1 WhatsApp sales channel app on Shopify for India, Malaysia, and Singapore, reflecting strong traction in the ecommerce vertical. Interakt has expanded its footprint into Instagram DMs and comments (launched 2025) and introduced AI Agents powered by Haptik's enterprise AI platform.

Interakt's strategic angle is democratizing enterprise-grade AI for India's SMB economy at aggressive price points — their "AI for All" initiative (September 2025) brings conversational AI agents to businesses starting at INR 10,000 (~$115), significantly undercutting traditional enterprise AI pricing. While historically a messaging and automation tool, Interakt is evolving toward agentic AI workflows on WhatsApp. Their core differentiation is affordability, deep Shopify/ecommerce integration, and the institutional power of the Reliance Jio ecosystem behind them.



## Key Features

### WhatsApp Calling

**Interakt has shipped native WhatsApp Business Calling as a generally available feature** (confirmed via https://www.interakt.shop/whatsapp-voice-calling/). This corrects earlier research that concluded calling was not yet productized. Key capabilities:

- Inbound and outbound WhatsApp voice calls from a unified business number
- Verified business identity displayed on all outgoing calls
- Mandatory customer opt-in / permission-based calling model (Meta compliance)
- Agent performance tracking and indicators
- Unified inbox integrating WhatsApp messages, WhatsApp calls, Instagram DMs, and comments
- **Call recordings included at no extra cost** (a direct differentiator they market against competitors)
- **AI transcripts and AI summaries automatically generated** (included free with calling)
- Available on **Advanced & CRM plans** (not on Starter or Growth)
- Per-minute calling charges apply for actual usage; no separate subscription fee for the feature
- Their comparison table explicitly highlights that call recordings and transcripts are free vs. traditional telephony providers that charge for these as paid add-ons

### Voice AI / Conversational AI

Interakt's Voice AI capabilities are delivered via Haptik's enterprise AI platform, available as a premium add-on. This is a meaningful offering but positioned differently from Wati's Astra (which is natively embedded in the WhatsApp channel).

- **Haptik Voice AI Agents** (available via Interakt as add-on, launched broadly September 2025 via "AI for All" initiative):
  - Inbound and outbound calling automation on phone/IVR channels
  - Replaces traditional IVRs with contextual, conversational voice AI
  - Handles support queries, bookings, follow-up calls, and lead qualification autonomously
  - Multilingual support: 22 Indian languages (Haptik's primary positioning); their enterprise platform claims 100+ languages
  - 24/7 availability, zero hold time
  - Seamless handoff to live agents with conversation summaries and intent detection
  - CRM integration for personalized interactions using past conversation data
  - Voice customization: tone, gender, accent, voice depth selection
  - Entry price: INR 10,000 (~$115) for SMBs via the "AI for All" initiative; cost per conversation INR 3–5
  - Reported outcomes: up to 80% of repetitive queries automated; 20–25% increase in lead-to-sale conversions; 30–40% reduction in manual support costs
- **WhatsApp AI Agents (Chat-based, not voice):**
  - FAQ bots, lead qualification bots, product recommendation bots
  - No-code deployment via Interakt dashboard
  - GPT-powered chatbots for WhatsApp conversations
  - Automated order tracking, appointment scheduling, feedback collection
- **Key distinction from Wati's Astra**: Haptik's Voice AI operates on traditional phone/IVR channels, not natively inside WhatsApp voice calls. It is a separate telephony-based product, not a WhatsApp Calling AI agent. No published evidence of a "Voice AI on WhatsApp Calling channel" feature comparable to Wati Astra.
- No publicly documented no-code voice AI builder specifically for WhatsApp voice calls
- No published live language switching during WhatsApp voice calls

### Core Platform Capabilities

- **Shared Team Inbox**: Multi-agent inbox on a single WhatsApp Business number; chat assignment, tagging, and collaboration across teams; mobile app available (Android/iOS)
- **Broadcast & Campaign Management**: Bulk WhatsApp message campaigns with audience segmentation (by purchase history, location, behavior); delivery and engagement rate tracking; Click-to-WhatsApp ad analytics dashboard
- **Automation & Workflows**: No-code chatbot builder with event-based workflows; quick reply buttons (up to 10 responses); automated triggers for abandoned cart recovery, order confirmations, shipping updates; integration with Shopify and WooCommerce for ecommerce automation
- **Ecommerce & Catalog Integration**: Auto-sync Shopify product catalog to WhatsApp; WhatsApp carts converted to Shopify orders; product recommendation AI; order management AI agent; #1 WhatsApp sales channel app on Shopify India/Malaysia/Singapore
- **Multi-Channel (Limited)**: WhatsApp (primary) + Instagram DMs and comments (added 2025); does not support email, SMS, Facebook Messenger as standalone channels; no voice as a standalone channel on the inbox
- **Integrations**: Shopify, WooCommerce, payment gateways, CRM systems; 100+ connectors claimed; Razorpay and other payment integrations for in-chat payments

### Notable Strengths

- Deepest Shopify/WhatsApp ecommerce integration in the Indian market; purpose-built for D2C commerce workflows
- Backed by Reliance Jio — institutional distribution, credibility, and potential for bundled sales through Jio's telecom and enterprise channels
- Haptik's enterprise AI platform (10+ years of AI/NLP experience) powering conversational and voice AI add-ons
- Most affordable entry-level pricing in the WhatsApp BSP market ($12/month USD); accessible for micro-SMBs
- "AI for All" initiative positions them as the democratizing force for AI in Indian SMB market, with government/ecosystem tailwinds from Reliance Jio

### Missing / Weak Areas (vs. Wati)

- WhatsApp Calling only available on **Advanced & CRM plans** — not on Starter or Growth, limiting access for smaller SMBs (Wati includes calling on all three paid plans)
- Thinner calling feature set vs. Wati: no documented call CTA buttons in templates, no missed call notifications, no business hours calling controls, no multi-number management, no call permission template localization
- Haptik Voice AI is a telephony/IVR product, not a WhatsApp-native voice AI agent — does not match Wati Astra's WhatsApp-embedded voice AI positioning
- No published no-code builder for AI voice call flows on WhatsApp
- Customer support widely criticized (6–7 day email response times per user reviews); direct contrast to Wati's sub-24-hour support
- Limited omnichannel scope (only WhatsApp + Instagram); no SMS, email, or other channels

## Target Market

Primary: Indian SMBs, MSMEs, and D2C/ecommerce brands (especially Shopify merchants) looking for affordable WhatsApp automation, broadcast campaigns, and basic conversational AI; companies where INR pricing and Reliance Jio ecosystem relationships matter

Secondary: Southeast Asian SMBs (Malaysia, Singapore) using Shopify + WhatsApp commerce; mid-market Indian businesses considering AI agent automation via Haptik's enterprise platform

Less focused on: Global SMBs outside India/Southeast Asia who need multi-channel support beyond WhatsApp + Instagram; businesses requiring native WhatsApp voice calling with analytics; enterprises needing complex omnichannel automation across email, SMS, and non-Meta channels



## Strengths

- Institutional backing from Reliance Jio ($100M acquisition, deep distribution network across India's SMB economy)
- Market-leading Shopify + WhatsApp ecommerce integration (#1 app in India, Malaysia, Singapore app stores)
- Largest AI/NLP pedigree of any WhatsApp BSP — Haptik has been building enterprise conversational AI since 2013, powering Voice AI and chat agents
- Most affordable pricing in the market ($12/month entry), enabling penetration into micro-SMBs
- "AI for All" initiative (September 2025) — strategically timed to capture India's AI adoption wave for SMBs before competitors
- Expanding from WhatsApp-only to WhatsApp + Instagram omnichannel (2025), widening their addressable market
- Strong ecommerce automation workflows (abandoned cart, order tracking, product recommendation AI) that are deeply integrated and no-code



## Weaknesses

- WhatsApp Business Calling is gated to Advanced & CRM plans only — Wati makes calling available on all paid plans including Growth
- Calling feature set is thinner: no call CTA buttons in templates, no missed call notifications, no business hours controls, no multi-number management vs. Wati's full calling suite
- Voice AI capability (Haptik) operates on telephony/IVR channels, not natively on WhatsApp voice calls — misaligned with where WhatsApp Business Calling API is heading
- Customer support widely criticized in G2 and Capterra reviews: email-only support with 6–7 day response times; this is a significant churn driver for SMBs needing responsive help
- Limited omnichannel coverage: only WhatsApp + Instagram; competitors like Wati and respond.io support more channels
- Platform UI/UX issues: users report slow system performance, needing frequent refreshes, and complex dashboard navigation
- Automation depth limited for complex use cases: basic chatbot builder with limited branching logic; not suitable for enterprise-scale workflows
- Primarily India/Southeast Asia focused — limited brand recognition, local support, and product localization outside these markets



## Comparison to Wati

### Where Interakt Wins:
- Lower entry-level pricing ($12/month vs Wati's higher SMB tiers) — stronger value for cost-sensitive Indian SMBs
- Deeper Shopify ecommerce integration and WhatsApp commerce catalog management, especially for D2C brands
- Haptik's enterprise AI pedigree and Voice AI for telephony/IVR use cases
- Backed by Reliance Jio, giving access to distribution at scale in India
- Instagram DMs and comments support (added 2025)
- Strong "AI for All" narrative and brand positioning for India's SMB AI adoption wave

### Where Wati Wins:
- **WhatsApp Calling breadth**: Wati includes calling on Growth, Pro, and Business plans; Interakt gates it to Advanced ($63/month) and above
- **Calling feature depth**: Wati has call CTA buttons in templates, missed call notifications, business hours controls, multi-number management, call permission template localization — none of these are documented on Interakt
- **Voice AI on WhatsApp**: Wati's Astra is natively embedded in WhatsApp voice calls; Interakt's Haptik Voice AI operates on traditional telephony, a fundamentally different (and less unified) UX
- **Multi-channel breadth**: Wati supports Web + WhatsApp + Voice in an integrated AI agent; Interakt is WhatsApp + Instagram only
- **No-code voice AI builder**: Wati Astra has a documented no-code builder for voice AI flows; Interakt has no equivalent on WhatsApp voice
- **Customer support quality**: Wati's sub-24-hour response vs. Interakt's documented 6–7 day email response times
- **Global reach**: Wati serves 15,000+ customers across 100+ countries; Interakt is primarily India/Southeast Asia
- **Live language switching on calls**: Wati Astra supports 30+ languages with live switching; Interakt's telephony Voice AI supports 22 Indian languages primarily
- **Automation depth**: Wati supports more complex chatbot flows (up to 200 automation steps)

### Strategic Positioning Against Interakt:
Both platforms now have WhatsApp Business Calling, but Wati's calling suite is deeper and available on more plans. Wati is the clear choice for businesses that need calling on a starter plan, need call CTA templates or missed call workflows, or want a WhatsApp-native AI voice agent — Interakt's Voice AI still runs on a separate telephony channel, not inside WhatsApp calls.

Win scenarios:
- SMBs on Growth/Pro plans who need WhatsApp Calling — Interakt requires the more expensive Advanced plan
- Any deal where the prospect wants call CTA buttons in templates, missed call notifications, or business hours call controls
- Any deal where the prospect wants a WhatsApp-native Voice AI agent (not a telephony IVR bolted on separately) — Wati Astra vs. Haptik Voice are fundamentally different products
- Global deals (outside India/Southeast Asia) where Interakt has limited localization and support
- Deals where customer support SLA matters — Interakt's documented 6–7 day email response is a liability
- Mid-market or growth-stage companies that need more complex automation (200+ step chatbots) and omnichannel beyond Meta platforms
- Businesses using WhatsApp Calling to replace or augment their phone support — Wati's call analytics and agent tracking suite has no Interakt equivalent

**Haptik Voice AI (telephony/IVR add-on — separate product, not WhatsApp calling):** Haptik's enterprise security and privacy documentation explicitly references "voice recordings, transcripts, and analytics" as data subject to role-based, least-privilege, and time-bound access controls with full auditability. ### AI Transcription & Summarization

**WhatsApp Business Calling (Interakt platform):** Not independently verifiable from public documentation as of March 2026. The prior update in this file claims AI transcripts and AI summaries are automatically generated and included free with calling. No public-facing documentation, help article, or third-party review corroborates this feature existing on the platform.

**Haptik Voice AI (telephony add-on):** Two AI-driven post-call capabilities are documented on haptik.ai:

1. **AI-generated conversation summary at agent handoff:** Explicitly documented — when a voice AI call escalates to a human agent, Haptik automatically provides the agent with an AI-generated conversation summary and detected intent. This is a handoff-assist feature, not a standalone post-call review tool for supervisors or quality assurance workflows.
2. **Transcripts:** Referenced in Haptik's security/privacy documentation as data that exists and is governed by access controls. No public documentation describes a searchable transcript viewer, downloadable transcript artifact, or supervisor-facing review interface. Whether transcripts are accessible to Interakt SMB customers in a self-serve way is not publicly documented.

No publicly documented features for: full call transcript library accessible to agents or supervisors, standalone post-call summary report UI, AI-based call QA scoring, or coaching tools derived from call transcripts — at least not in any publicly documented SMB-accessible product tier.

### Other GenAI on Voice

**Sentiment analysis:** Haptik's analytics dashboard tracks sentiment as a metric across voice interactions. Platform-level analytics feature applied post-conversation; not a real-time in-call coaching tool.

**Intent detection:** Documented at the handoff point — the voice AI passes detected customer intent to the receiving human agent. Not documented as a post-call reportable field in a supervisor-facing review interface.

**RAG-powered knowledge during calls:** Haptik Voice AI agents use Retrieval-Augmented Generation against enterprise knowledge bases (PDFs, DOCX, CSVs, websites) to generate responses during the AI-handled portion of telephony calls. GenAI feature applied during the call, not as a post-call analytics layer.

**No documented features for:** real-time transcription visible to supervisors during live calls, AI coaching nudges during agent-handled calls, call highlight extraction, or GenAI-based QA scoring of agent-handled calls in any publicly documented Interakt SMB product tier.
