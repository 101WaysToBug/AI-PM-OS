# Competitor Brief: AiSensy

Last Updated: March 2026 (re-verified)
Analyst: Wati Product Team



## Overview

AiSensy is an India-based WhatsApp Business API platform founded in 2020 by Gautam Rajesh Shelley, Mohit Dua, and Ajay Rathore, and headquartered in Gurugram (Haryana). The company has grown to serve 100,000+ businesses across 57 countries and reported annual revenue of approximately ₹79.6 Cr (~$9.5M USD) as of March 31, 2025. It has raised funding from BlueLotus Ventures, Dream Green Capital, and Mars Shot Ventures, though the total funding amount is not publicly disclosed. With 100 employees as of April 2025 (195% year-over-year headcount growth), AiSensy is scaling rapidly and competes primarily on price and ease of onboarding in the SMB segment.

AiSensy positions itself as the "Smartest WhatsApp Marketing Platform," with a strong emphasis on broadcast campaigns, Click-to-WhatsApp advertising, and AI-powered chatbot automation. Its primary competitive angle is offering official WhatsApp Business API access at a lower price point than most competitors, including a Forever Free plan, making it attractive to cost-conscious SMBs and eCommerce businesses in India and emerging markets. The platform has been aggressively adding AI capabilities throughout 2024–2025, including AI Agents, an AI chatbot flow generator, and most recently, an AI Voice Calling product.

From a competitive standpoint, AiSensy is a relevant Wati challenger in the SMB and mid-market segments, particularly in India. However, it trails Wati significantly on enterprise-grade team workflows, deep CRM integrations, native WhatsApp Business Calling (Meta API-based), and a mature Voice AI offering. Its strategy appears to be winning on price and breadth of AI tooling rather than depth of any single capability.

### Core Platform Capabilities

- **WhatsApp Broadcasting and Retargeting**: Bulk campaign broadcasts with smart audience segmentation, scheduled campaigns, and retargeting based on campaign engagement (read, clicked, not replied, etc.). Campaign click tracking available on Pro and above plans.
- **Multi-Agent Team Inbox**: Shared inbox allowing multiple human agents to manage conversations from one WhatsApp number; includes chat assignment, agent transfer, manager monitoring, notes, tags, and response SLA management. Up to 1 Owner + 5 agents included free; additional agents at $20/month (USD) or ₹750/month (INR).
- **No-Code Chatbot Flow Builder**: Drag-and-drop chatbot builder; AI can auto-generate chatbot flows based on business goals. AI WhatsApp Chatbot uses NLP and machine learning for intent understanding and context-aware responses.
- **Click-to-WhatsApp Ads**: Run Facebook/Instagram ads that launch WhatsApp conversations, with integrated ROAS and conversion tracking (on Pro plans). This is a notable differentiator vs. some competitors.
- **Integrations**: 2,000+ integrations via Zapier and native connectors; direct integrations include Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Sheets, and payment gateways. Shopify integration includes abandoned cart recovery, order notifications, and delivery updates.
- **AI Agents on WhatsApp**: Deployed WhatsApp AI Agents that automate end-to-end customer conversations, handle orders, schedule appointments, process payments, and escalate to humans; supports text, voice notes, images, and video on WhatsApp.
